Why did I choose this project?

I wanted to share with everyone my favorite way to cut watermelons, especially for a large group of people. We would cut watermelons this way at my summer camp growing up and I think it is the best way to cut watermelon.


Other Good Methods

Cutting watermelons into wedges is also a great method for when you want larger pieces of watermelon. A quick description on how to do this - cut the watermelons in half, longways. Cut them again, into quarters, and then into 1 inch sliced wedges.

Enjoy!
