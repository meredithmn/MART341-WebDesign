The best way to cut watermelons

Instructions

Step 1 - Gather Materials

  • Ripe Watermelon
  • Sharp knife



Step 2 - Cut watermelon in half

Cut the watermelon in half so that you have two domes Place the domes face down on your cutting board

TIP* Cutting longways is better for wedges




Step 3 - Cut pieces

Cut 1 inch strips in your watermelon dome, forming a grid pattern of readily available watermelon sticks
