Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Golden Potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • garlic powder (or garlic salt)

HOW TO:

  1. Preheat over to 425oF
  2. Peel potatoes (optional*)
  3. Chop potatoes into small squares
  4. Cover baking sheet in tin foil & coat with olive oil
  5. Put potatoes on baking sheet & toss with the oil
  6. Add garlic powder or garlic salt to taste
  7. Bake in oven until golden brown (about 15 minutes)
  8. Remove from oven & sprinkle with salt while still hot
